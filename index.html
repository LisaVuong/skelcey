Artist Statement

My work is often a reaction to the world around me; things, people, and events manifest themselves through shape and material experimentation, technique, and perserverance. It is driven by exploration of technique and 3D computer modeling and 3D printing processes. I take what shockes, arouses, inspires, and horrifies in the world and create forms that reflect a distortion of these influences into a thoughtful and compelling body of work.

The Missing Bees

Upon getting this assignment of Past | Present | Future one of the first ideas that came to mind was the mystery of the disappearing honeybee. The first thing that you think about a honeybee is that it is the producer of honey. This however is merely the tip of the iceberg. The honeybee is the main pollinator for most vegetables, nuts, and fruit; bees pollinate one third of the nations produce. This disappearance has affected 24 U.S. states already and is also an international problem as well.
